Reuben Sandwich 2

Description

"These sandwiches are really delicious and easy to make. They are one of my family's fix-it-quick favorites. I like to serve them with big bowls of steaming vegetable soup and dill pickles on the side. Enjoy!" - allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet or griddle on medium heat.
  2. Lightly butter one side of bread slices.
  3. Spread non-buttered sides with Thousand Island dressing.
  4. On 4 bread slices, layer 1 slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut and second slice of Swiss cheese.
  5. Top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides out.
  6. Grill sandwiches until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
